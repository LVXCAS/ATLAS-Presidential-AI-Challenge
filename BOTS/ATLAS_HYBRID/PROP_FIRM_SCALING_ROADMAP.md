## Risk Management Across Accounts

**Critical Rule:** Never correlate trades across accounts

If you run identical ATLAS instances on:
- Your $2.7M personal account
- 19 prop firm accounts ($8.44M total)

And ATLAS has a bad week, **all 20 accounts lose simultaneously**. This destroys the diversification benefit.

### Solution: Asset Class Segregation

**Your Personal Account ($2.7M):**
- 25% Forex
- 25% Options
- 25% Futures
- 25% Crypto

**Prop Firm Accounts (by firm type):**
- Aqua Funded (6 accounts): Forex only (cTrader platform)
- The5ers (4 accounts): Forex only (different pairs)
- FunderPro (4 accounts): Futures only (ES, NQ, CL, GC)
- FXIFY (2 accounts): Forex only (DXTrade)
- Instant Funding (3 accounts): Options only (stock spreads)

**Result:** If crypto crashes 40%, your personal account takes a 10% hit (25% allocation), but all 19 prop accounts are unaffected. True diversification.
