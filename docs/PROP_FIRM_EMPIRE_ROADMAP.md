## üõ°Ô∏è RISK MANAGEMENT ACROSS MULTIPLE ACCOUNTS

### Account-Level Risk Management
- **Max Daily Loss:** 3% per account (most firms require this)
- **Max Total Drawdown:** 5-10% per account (trailing)
- **Position Sizing:** 0.5-1% risk per trade
- **Max Concurrent Trades:** 3 per account

### Portfolio-Level Risk Management
- **Diversification:** FOREX + FUTURES + CRYPTO (uncorrelated)
- **Account Staggering:** Don't deploy all accounts on same day
- **Capital Preservation:** If account hits 50% of max drawdown, reduce position sizes
- **Profit Protection:** Withdraw 50% of profits monthly, reinvest 50%

### System-Level Safeguards
- **Kill Switch:** Auto-stop all trading if portfolio down 10% in one day
- **Weekend Closures:** Close all FOREX positions Friday 1 PM PST (most firms require)
- **News Filter:** Avoid trading during NFP, FOMC, CPI releases
- **Performance Monitoring:** Daily P/L tracking across all accounts
